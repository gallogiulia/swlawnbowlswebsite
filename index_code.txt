<h2>NEWS FLASH:  CHANGE OF LOCATION, DATE &amp; TIME OF Men's AGM to January 6th, 2019 at 9:30 a.m. at Laguna Woods</h2>

<p>Please be advised that the City of Arcadia has informed the Santa Anita LBC that the clubhouse will be unavailable for the previously planned AGM due to construction. There are not that many sites that are suitable for a large gathering, but thanks to Laguna Wood’s President Al DeSantis we have been able to secure Laguna Woods Club&nbsp;House&nbsp;2 for our meeting. The only availability was on Sunday January&nbsp;6,&nbsp;2019 between 9a.m. to noon. <strong>Thus the new location will be Laguna&nbsp;Woods Lawn Bowling Club, Club House 2. Utilize the same entrance as you do for going to the lawn bowling club, Gate 12</strong>. For those that are going to attend it would be helpful if you can <a href="mailto:gdayton@me.com">email secretary Gary Barnes</a> with the names of the two representatives that will be in attendance representing your club, so your names can be left at the entrance gate. We will also ask the gate attendant to let people in that indicate they are attending the AGM for lawn bowling. Due to a tight time window the <strong>meeting will start at 9:30 a.m</strong>. The meeting is also open to all members that wish to attend. Any questions feel free to call me at 310&#8209;780&#8209;6927. President, Phil Dunn</p>

<h3>Pasadena To Host its First Annual Rose Lawn Bowl - December 31st<br></h3>
<p>We will be holding the first annual Rose Lawn  Bowl at the Historic Greens of Pasadena the day before the Rose Bowl  Game.  <br>
It's open to alumni  and parents of University of Georgia and Oklahoma University.<br>
<br>
There will be 9 slots for each school. We will play 3 triples games. The team  who wins 2 of them, is the champion. We expect local TV, Radio and newspapers  to be covering the event. </p>
<p>We're also reaching  out to the alumni associations of the two schools. All talent levels are  welcome. We will be giving lessons in the days leading up to the event for  those who have never bowled. </p>
<p>The game will take  place 10am on Sunday December 31st. We have a new permanent trophy. As of now,  the only prize is bragging rights, but in the future we plan to hook up with a  charity and award medals. The good news is: This year IT'S FREE!</p>

<p>Interested?  contact PLBC Secretary Lee Patrick Sullivan - <a href="mailto:leepatricksullivan@hotmail.com">leepatricksullivan@hotmail.com</a></p>

<h2>Alhambra News <span class="font_12 font_regular"><em>(from Cheri Cabot's Facebook post)</em></span></h2>
<p>September 27 - WE WON!! It has been an emotional and difficult 9 months, but we won! We   will be losing one of our greens, and that is fine, but here is the   good news; we will be getting a newly refurbished green that has now a   carpeted green that was destroyed by the city. New natural turf green,   new sprinkler system (that actually works)  and lights!! We will be   getting lights that actually work. The entire area around our greens   will be refurbished and landscaped. New and additional basketball courts   (not right next to us fortunately) and in the area that is currently   our natural turf green will be picnic tables, benches and shaded areas.   What a relief. <strong>We will continue to have lawn bowling in Alhambra!</strong></p>
